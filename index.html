About the artist

Music for the spaces between moments.

Hi, I’m Romanich — an independent musician based in South Korea. I create ambient, lo-fi, piano and instrumental music inspired by nature, memories, city walks and quiet moments.

Short biography

Romanich is an independent musician based in Seoul, South Korea, creating lo-fi, ambient, piano-led instrumentals and original songs. His work turns city walks, changing weather, quiet evenings and everyday routines into warm, atmospheric music.

Selected achievements

  • A growing 60-track SoundCloud catalog spanning lo-fi, electronic and ambient music.
  • Original music videos, lyric videos and live busking performances published from South Korea.
  • Independent releases distributed across SoundCloud, Bandcamp, Spotify, Apple Music and YouTube.
